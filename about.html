Will “The Fresh Prince”
is a street‑smart teenager from West Philadelphia - “born and raised”. While playing street basketball, Will misses a shot and the ball hits a group of gang members, causing a confrontation that frightens his mother, who sends him to live with his wealthy aunt and uncle in the opulent neighborhood of Bel‑Air.

The Cast

Starring Will Smith with leading role and supporting cast James Avery, Daphne Reid, Alfonso Ribeiro, Karyn Parsons, Tatyana Ali, Joseph Marcell and Ross Bagley.
